Privacy Policy – Bible Hangman

Last updated: May 24, 2025

Developer: Cristais Studios

1. Data Collection

The Bible Hangman app may collect the following types of user data:

  • Personal Data: name and email address (if voluntarily provided by the user).
  • Technical Data: device information, operating system version, anonymous identifiers (such as device ID), crash logs, and app usage data.
  • Sensitive Data: we do not collect sensitive personal information such as racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs (beyond biblical-themed content), political opinions, biometrics, or health data.

Note: The app contains Christian and biblical content but does not require users to disclose their religion or any sensitive data.

2. Use of Data

Data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve the user's in-app experience;
  • To fix bugs and enhance performance;
  • To send app-related communications (if the user opts in);
  • To comply with legal obligations or regulations.

3. Data Sharing

Cristais Studios does not sell or share user data for commercial purposes. Data may be shared with:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Firebase) for monitoring and improving the app;
  • Cloud storage platforms used for secure data hosting;
  • Legal authorities if required by law or court order.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, destruction, loss, alteration, or unlawful processing. These measures include:

  • Secure data transmission via TLS/SSL encryption;
  • Restricted access to data by authorized personnel only;
  • Storage systems secured against external threats.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retention: Collected data is stored as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law.
  • Deletion: Users can request deletion of their personal data by contacting our support. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, in accordance with legal obligations.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about them;
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information;
  • Request deletion of their personal data;
  • Withdraw consent previously given.

For any privacy-related request, please contact: contato@cristaisstudios.com

Cristais Studios reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, with notice provided within the app or through other appropriate means. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for updates.
